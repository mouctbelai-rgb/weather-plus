Slightly increases the game's difficulty by adding new weather conditions for Mindustry! Each weather event makes the game more challenging in its own way.

=== Available in the mod ===

1. Acid downpour:
Corrodes unit armor and applies a damage-over-time effect.

2. Meteor shower:
Hurls actual burning comets onto the map! Strikes random buildings.

3. Ion storm:
Causes widespread short circuits in power grids. Strikes batteries and factories with clusters of blue chain lightning, inflicting EMP shock on units.

4. Cryo Storm:
An extreme polar blizzard. It freezes all coal, steam, and differential generators solid until the storm passes.
